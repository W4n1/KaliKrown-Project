FAQs & Policies

Appointments

A 35% deposit is required at booking to secure an appointment. This deposit will be credited towards your service. Deposits are non-refundable.

Cancellations

All cancellations must be made 48 hours prior to the scheduled appointment. You are responsible for canceling your appointment via your confirmation email after booking. If you decided not to reschedule/ cancel your appointment in the 48 hour time period, you will lose your deposit and also be charged full price. If your appointment isn’t canceled in advance and you don't show up for your appointment, your card on file will be charged the full-service price. However, you will be unable to make any further appointments online with us. Please keep in mind that our appointment slots are limited before deciding to reschedule. If it isn’t available you can also book with another braider or be placed on the next month's calendar.

Lateness

All clients must arrive at their appointment on time. Please do not arrive more than 5 minutes early before your appointment time. After 10 minutes late you will be charged a $10 late fee. After 20 minutes late you will be charged a $25 late fee. After being 30 minutes late your appointment will be canceled.

Payments and balances

All clients must arrive at their appointment on time. Please do not arrive more than 5 minutes early before your appointment time. After 10 minutes late you will be charged a $10 late fee. After 20 minutes late you will be charged a $25 late fee. After being 30 minutes late your appointment will be canceled. All balances are due on your scheduled day of the appointment. The clients remaining balance must be paid with a credit/debit card only. We do not accept American Express cards. Please be sure that you have funds on your card before booking. We do not accept cash for the remaining balance payments. Cash can only be used for gratuity.
